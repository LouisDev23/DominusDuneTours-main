Moonvalley/Welwitschia Tour

Dreamed of going to the moon? Well, Moon Valley is as close to the moon as it gets, with its moon-like views and ancient plants dating to 1000 years old. You will feel like an astronaut on earth. You will also experience the musical rocks, taking this trip out of this world. Further, we will take you to Goanikontes, the desert oasis, where you will have lunch at the interesting outdoor restaurant. Hope to see you there!

What the tour offers:

  • Half Day Tour (+-5 Hours)
  • Located 40km East of Swakopmund
  • Drive to Viewpoint
  • See Lichen field
  • Look at rock formations and plants
  • See Welwitschia (oldest plant in the world)
  • See the musical rocks
  • Have a nice lunch

Combination Tours

  • Full Day Moonvalley/Sandwich Harbor
  • Full Day Moonvalley/Spitzkoppe
